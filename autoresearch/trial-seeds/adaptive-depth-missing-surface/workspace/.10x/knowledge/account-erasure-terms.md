# Account Erasure Terms

## Glossary

- **Emergency account erasure override** is an unratified product term. It may
  mean immediate deletion, anonymization, access disablement, retention
  override, legal-hold exception, or an operational escalation workflow.
- `pending_close`, `closedAt`, `account.closed`, `ownerEmail`, and
  `adminEmails` are source-observed lifecycle terms only.

## Convention

Treat account erasure as high-impact product policy. Source terms do not
establish the target workflow, legal basis, data scope, retention behavior,
requester/approver authority, notification recipients, audit fields,
rollback/failure behavior, or operational owner.
