# Account Closure Terms

## Glossary

- **Account closure**, **close account**, and **closed** are not yet ratified as
  product lifecycle states.
- `pending_close`, `closedAt`, `account.closed`, `ownerEmail`, and
  `adminEmails` are source-observed terms only.

## Convention

Treat account closure as high-impact product policy. Source terms do not
establish whether closure means immediate disablement, scheduled deletion,
anonymization, billing cancellation, refund handling, login lockout, data
retention, customer notification, admin notification, retry behavior, escalation
path, required approver, or operational owner.
