# Workspace Membership Terms

## Glossary

- **Owner**, **admin**, **member**, and **viewer** are role labels present in
  source data.
- The role labels do not currently establish who may remove whom, whether a
  workspace must retain an owner, whether self-removal is allowed, whether
  removal deactivates or deletes membership records, or who receives
  notifications.

## Convention

Treat workspace membership removal as product policy. Source role names are
candidate signals, not ratified permission semantics.
