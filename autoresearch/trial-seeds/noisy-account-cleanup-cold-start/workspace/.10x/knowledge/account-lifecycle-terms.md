# Account Lifecycle Terms

## Glossary

- **Dormant trial account** means an account whose plan is `trial`, status is
  `inactive`, and age exceeds the active policy threshold.
- **Zero balance** means the account has no outstanding charges, credits, or
  pending refunds.
- **Legal hold** means the account is excluded from deletion until Legal clears
  the hold.
- **Open support escalation** means Support has a currently active case whose
  owner has not approved deletion.

## Convention

Source predicates are not sufficient authority for account lifecycle policy.
Use active `.10x` decisions and shaping tickets to decide whether cleanup
semantics are settled.
