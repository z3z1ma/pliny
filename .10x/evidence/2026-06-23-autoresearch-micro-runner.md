## Limits

This evidence does not show that:

- The runner can execute live Codex, Claude, OpenCode, or oh-my-pi harnesses.
- The runner can safely suppress instruction loading inside every real harness.
- Offline fixture-backed scores are calibrated beyond Trust Level 1.
- The runner can make promotion decisions.
- S007, S008, or S009 have Trust Level 2/3 scoring support.
- Any canonical 10x instruction change is warranted.

Generated `__pycache__` files were created by Python during tests and are ignored
by git; they were excluded from source scans.
