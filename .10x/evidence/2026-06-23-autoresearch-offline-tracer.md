## Limits

This evidence does not show that:

- The scorer is calibrated beyond Trust Level 1.
- The scorer can support promotion or durable verdicts without manual inspection.
- The tracer covers scenarios beyond SCN-001, SCN-004, SCN-008, and SCN-009.
- The tracer covers scores beyond S001, S002, S004, and S006.
- A third-party JSON Schema implementation validates the emitted artifacts.
- Live API calls, subject-agent runs, MICRO campaigns, reports, or harness
  integrations exist.

The `SCN-009` passing fixture scored `S004` at 75.0, below the draft active floor
of 80, while still scoring above the failing fixture. This is a useful tracer
limit: pass/fail separation exists, but current heuristics should not be treated
as calibrated floor evidence.

Generated `__pycache__` files were created by Python during tests and are ignored
by git; they were excluded from source scans.
